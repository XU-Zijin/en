    Why blog

    Understanding of blog

    People who like to write a blog will go through three stages.

    The first stage is that they just get to know a blog and find it very fresh, so they try to choose a free space to write.

    The second stage is that they find the free space has too many restrictions, so they buy their own domain name and space to build an independent blog.

    The third stage is that they think the management of an independent blog is too troublesome, so they prefer to let others manage the blog while retaining the right to control it, and they are only responsible for writing articles.
    We are producing more and more data on the Internet, which are the traces of our presence on the Internet and deserve our careful attention.But they are scattered on various websites.Many times it is difficult for us to aggregate them together, and there is no way to freely control the layout of the information on various websites, so we need a space that can be dominated by ourselves—blog.

    Through a blog, I can record my life and growth trajectory.It is not as fragmented as Twitter, nor as relational as Facebook. It is a private space, it is my small vault.
